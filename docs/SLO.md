#Service Level Objectives

##api-gateway-service:
###Latency SLO: - 95% of API requests complete within **200 ms**.
###Reliability SLO: - **99.95% availability** per month.

##request-service:
###Latency SLO: - 95% of emergency request submissions and status lookups complete within **300 ms**.
###Reliability SLO: - **99.9% availability** per month, with no loss of submitted emergency requests.

##volunteer-service:
###Latency SLO: - 95% of volunteer registration, assignment, and availability updates complete within **500 ms**.
###Reliability SLO: - **99.9% availability** per month.

##dispatch-service:
###Latency SLO: - 95% of dispatch and resource assignment operations complete within **1 second**.
###Reliability SLO: - **99.99% availability** per month, ensuring emergency dispatch requests are processed without data loss.
